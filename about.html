Why This Site Exists

I spent 5 years paying shop rates for stuff I could have fixed myself. Flat tires, dead locks, busted welders, electrical problems — I threw money at all of it because I thought I needed a pro.

Then I got broke. Like, really broke. And I realized nobody was coming to save me. So I started watching YouTube, buying cheap tools, and burning my hands until I figured it out.

Turns out, most repairs are not that hard. The hard part is knowing which tool to buy and where to start. That is what this site is for.

What You Get Here:
  • Honest tool reviews — only stuff I have used or researched hard
  • Step-by-step repair guides written like a friend talking, not a manual
  • Amazon affiliate links so I can keep the lights on while you get the best price

I am not a certified mechanic or a master welder. I am a regular person who learned the hard way so you do not have to. If I can fix it, you can fix it too.
